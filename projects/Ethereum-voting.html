Ethereum Voting System

For my final year dissertation I developed a blockchain-based voting system using Ethereum smart contracts. This project pushed me to learn Solidity, improve my front-end skills, and understand how decentralised systems handle security and transparency.

How it works

The system operates across three main phases:

Initialisation

Users register with login credentials, a secret passphrase, and their wallet address. An admin reviews and verifies each user before voting begins.

Voting

The smart contract is deployed and opened by the admin. Verified users log in and cast their vote securely on the blockchain.

Results

Once all votes are cast, the contract closes automatically. Results are calculated and distributed to users.

Why this is useful

Blockchain technology enables a high level of transparency and security. All votes are publicly verifiable, while identities remain protected through wallet addresses, making the system both trustworthy and privacy-conscious.
